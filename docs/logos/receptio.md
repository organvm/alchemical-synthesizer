# Receptio — The Reception

> *The account of the constructed polis. Who the instrument is for, how it is
> met, and what it asks of the one who meets it.*

## The Audience the Instrument Constructs

An instrument does not merely have users; it *constructs* an audience by the
demands it makes. The Alchemical Synthesizer addresses three constituencies, in
descending order of intimacy:

1. **The Performer-as-Donor.** The deepest mode of reception is to *be absorbed*.
   The instrument is designed for players willing to feed it their own gear,
   their own signal, their own identity — and to be changed by what comes back.
   The closing maxim is an instruction to this audience: *learn when to let the
   instrument consume you back.*

2. **The Modular Patcher.** Those who arrive through the three control surfaces —
   the SuperCollider IDE, the Pure Data canvas, the Web Cortex — and treat
   Brahma as a 300-module rack with an unusually opinionated philosophy. They
   are met with familiar affordances (CV routing, sequencing, FX) wrapped around
   an unfamiliar premise (every module is an organism with a life cycle).

3. **The Reader-Theorist.** Those who encounter the work first as *idea* — the
   ethics of contamination, FSAP, the seven-stage organism — through the design
   precedent in `docs/design/` and this Logos layer. For them the instrument is
   an argument about identity, made in code.

## How It Is Met

- **Honestly.** The [pragma.md](pragma.md) surface refuses to oversell:
  measurement is partly placeholder, boot is multi-step, trait extraction is
  shallow. Reception is built on stated seams rather than concealed ones.
- **Visibly.** The Web Cortex renders organism state in real time — coherence as
  radius, entropy as jitter, type as colour — so that the act of contamination
  is *watched*, not hidden behind a faceplate.
- **In lineage.** The instrument never hides its sources. Rogue, The Thing,
  Relinquished, Cell, Ditto, Agent Smith are cited, not laundered. To receive
  Brahma is to receive its ancestors.

## What It Asks

The instrument asks for **contact** and returns **contamination**. The implicit
contract of reception:

- Bring a source. The instrument has no voice of its own to admire.
- Accept the Single-Equip discipline — focus over hoarding.
- Trust the IMMUNE governor as the last word before output, and the reflection
  logic as the instrument's right to defend itself.

## Current Standing

Within the ORGANVM polis, this repository is **ORGAN-II (Art)**, status
GRADUATED. Its declared edges: it *consumes* theory from ORGAN-I and *produces*
creative-artifacts for ORGAN-III (Commerce). Its Trivium dialect is
**AESTHETIC_FORM** — "The Poetry: proves formal structures have sensory form."
That is the precise term of its reception: a formal system (state machines,
trait validation, OSC topology) asked to *sound like something*.

The community and content pillars sit at *conception* (`ecosystem/pillar-dna/`).
Reception beyond the author is therefore still mostly **anticipated** rather than
recorded — and this page will be rewritten as real encounters accrue.
